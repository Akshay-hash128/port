Microbit Extension for H.I.R.O Lab

Optimized for cost reduction and performance improvement

Project Details

This project involved developing a custom microbit motor driver extension for the Human Interactive Robotics Lab (H.I.R.O) at Purdue. The design reduced pin wastage by 70% and projected a 40% reduction in production costs.
