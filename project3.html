Spherical Gear

The Abenics spherical gear is a revolutionary mechanical component that enables precise and flexible rotational movement in multiple directions.

Unlike traditional gears, which are limited to a single axis of rotation, the Abenics spherical gear can rotate in any direction, providing unparalleled versatility and adaptability.
