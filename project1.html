Low-Cost Cycloidal Gearbox

20:1 reduction ratio using NEMA 17 motors

Project Details

This project involved the development of a low-cost cycloidal gearbox, designed with a 20:1 reduction ratio and NEMA 17 stepper motors. The project applied Design for Manufacturing (DFM) principles and 3D printing optimization to reduce material costs and improve manufacturing efficiency.
